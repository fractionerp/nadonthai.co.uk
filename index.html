Welcome to Nadon Thai Restaurants


From Chiang Mai in northern Thailand, Nadon Thai serves modern Thai cuisine from our three locations in the North East since 2010.

We started our family owned business in Morpeth in 2010 with our first restaurant and following its success we opened two more restaurants.

All our chefs are Thai and have many years of experience in Thai cuisine. Known as the land of smiles, Thailand is famous for its food and hospitality, which we hope you will experience when visiting us.
